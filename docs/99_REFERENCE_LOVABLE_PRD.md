# Polus LLC — IT + Operations Consulting Website (Lovable.dev)  
**PRD v1.0 (Build-Ready)**  
**Primary market:** Oklahoma (startups + small businesses; secondary: construction, nonprofits)  
**Platform:** lovable.dev (build from scratch)  
**Payments:** Stripe (guest checkout supported)  
**Ecosystem:** Microsoft 365 (no Google Workspace)

---

## 1) Product Summary

Polus LLC needs a lead-generation website that communicates a clear value proposition, builds trust without relying on testimonials, and converts visitors into qualified leads via:
- **Free Discovery Call bookings**
- **Quote requests**
- **Lead magnet download**
- **Stripe payment links** (guest pay for deposits/invoices/packages)

Polus positions itself as the **friendly, professional, plain-English partner** that brings order to business operations and IT—**process + systems + implementation**.

---

## 2) Goals & Success Metrics

### Business goals
- Generate consistent qualified inbound leads in Oklahoma
- Convert visitors with clear packages + starting-at pricing
- Reduce sales friction from inquiry → proposal → payment
- Establish credibility via frameworks, certifications, and outcomes (without testimonials)

### Success metrics (track from launch)
- Conversion rate: (bookings + form submissions + downloads) / sessions
- Qualified lead rate (meets basic criteria: size/need/urgency)
- Time-to-first-response (SLA compliance)
- Lead → proposal rate, proposal → close rate
- Stripe payment link usage (deposit/package)

---

## 3) Target Users & Personas

### Persona A — Small business owner
- Wants reliability, fewer headaches, clear pricing, fast response
- Needs help “making it all work” without hiring full-time IT/ops staff

### Persona B — Operations lead / office manager
- Needs SOPs, onboarding, workflow clarity, better handoffs, fewer interruptions

### Persona C — Startup founder
- Needs MVP planning/execution, lightweight process, M365 structure, systems that scale

## 5) Offer Strategy

### Free services (customer experience, time-capped)
**Rules:** free = advice + next steps, not implementation; strict time caps.
- **Free Discovery Call (15–20 min)**
- **Free 1-page “Next Steps” recap** (template-based; sent within 24 hours)  
  - What I heard  
  - Top 3 priorities  
  - Recommended package(s) + starting-at price  
- **Free Process + IT Readiness Checklist** (1-page PDF lead magnet)

### Front-door paid offers (high-converting, fixed-scope)
1) **Business + IT Systems Assessment (90 min)** — Starting at **$249–$499**
2) **Process Mapping + SOP Starter Pack** — Starting at **$1,250–$3,500**
3) **IT Stability + Backup/DR Check** — Starting at **$399–$899**

### Core paid services (resume-aligned)
**Microsoft 365 + Endpoint + Identity**
- Identity & Access Baseline (MFA, admin separation, access review cadence)
- Onboarding/Offboarding System Build (checklist + access map + SOPs)
- Endpoint Standardization (policy baseline, compliance approach, standard app set)
- M365 Tenant Cleanup + Governance (Teams/SharePoint/OneDrive structure + permissions sanity)

**Reliability & Continuity**
- Backup Verification + Restore Test
- DR Runbook + Quarterly Restore Cadence

**Service Operations (ITSM, not Jira-focused)**
- Service Desk Setup (platform-agnostic): intake forms, ticket categories, SLAs, KB starter, reporting  
  *(Implementation may use a selected SMB-friendly tool; do not position “Jira-first” on the site.)*

**Operations Systemization**
- SOP library build-out + training
- Asset inventory + lifecycle plan
- Vendor & tool rationalization (reduce spend + complexity)

**Web & MVP**
- Small business website builds (lead-gen)
- MVP/PRD kickoff package (planning + first iteration)

### Engagement models
- **Packages (fixed scope / fixed price)** = default
- **Hourly** = for custom work, overflow, ongoing support

## 9) Page Requirements (Lovable build specs)

### 9.1 Home
**Purpose:** immediate clarity + trust + conversion  
**Must include sections**
- Hero (positioning + CTA + microcopy)
- “What we do” (3 category cards)
- “How it works” (3 steps)
- Packages preview (front-door offers + starting-at)
- Trust signals (certifications + approach)
- Lead magnet block (checklist download)
- FAQ preview (3–5)
- Final CTA

**Acceptance criteria**
- Primary CTA visible above the fold on desktop and mobile
- At least 2 conversion points before footer (book + download or quote)

---

### 9.2 Services Overview
**Purpose:** browseable catalog with outcomes + pricing  
**Must include**
- Category filter (Ops, M365/IT, Reliability/DR, Web/MVP, Automation)
- Service cards with:
  - 1-sentence outcome
  - 3 bullet deliverables
  - “Starting at” price
  - CTA: Book / Quote

**Acceptance criteria**
- Every card links to a detail page using a shared template

---

### 9.3 Service Detail Page Template
**Required sections**
- Who it’s for
- Problems we solve
- What you get (deliverables)
- Timeline (typical)
- Pricing (starting at + what affects cost)
- FAQ (5–8)
- CTA block (“Book a Free Discovery Call” + “Request a Quote”)

**Acceptance criteria**
- Consistent layout + headings across all service pages
- CTA and contact path visible without heavy scrolling

---

### 9.4 Industries Pages
**Purpose:** relevance + local SEO  
**Required**
- Top pains in that industry
- Recommended services mapped to pains
- Sample deliverables (generic)
- CTA

**Acceptance criteria**
- No compliance claims unless verified; use “can help you prepare for” language where needed
