# PRD — Polus LLC Website (Copilot Build) v1.0

Stack: **Next.js App Router + TypeScript + Tailwind**.

## Product Summary
Polus LLC needs a lead-generation website for Oklahoma startups and small businesses that converts visitors into leads via:
- **Book a Free Discovery Call**
- Quote requests
- Lead magnet download
- Stripe payment links (guest pay)

Constraints:
- Microsoft 365 ecosystem only
- ITSM is platform-agnostic (not Jira-first)
- Blog/resources disabled in v1
- No unverified compliance claims

## Primary CTA
Button text: **Book a Free Discovery Call**  
Microcopy: “Free • 15–20 minutes • You’ll leave with next steps”

## Offers
Free:
- Discovery call (15–20 min)
- 1-page Next Steps recap (template) within 24 hours
- Process + IT Readiness Checklist (1-page PDF)

Front-door paid:
- Business + IT Systems Assessment (90 min) — starting at $249–$499
- Process Mapping + SOP Starter Pack — starting at $1,250–$3,500
- IT Stability + Backup/DR Check — starting at $399–$899
