# Polus LLC — Brand Guide (v1.0)

This brand guide defines **how Polus sounds, what Polus promises, and how Polus builds trust**.  
(For UI tokens and components, see the separate **Style Guide JSON**.)

---

## Brand snapshot
- **Positioning:** Polus helps Oklahoma small businesses run smoother by fixing broken processes and strengthening IT—so you get more time, less chaos, and fewer surprises.
- **Personality:** Professional, Friendly, Clear, Reliable, Modern
- **Primary CTA:** **Book a Free Discovery Call**
- **CTA microcopy:** Free • 15–20 minutes • You’ll leave with next steps

---

## Target audience
**Primary:** Oklahoma startups and small businesses (owners, ops managers)  
**Secondary:** Construction and nonprofits

Polus wins when the buyer wants:
- clear next steps (easy to understand)
- predictable pricing
- stability and documentation
- a partner who follows through

---

## Messaging pillars
1) **Plain-English clarity** — clear options, clear scope, clear “starting at” pricing  
2) **Systems that stick** — SOPs + training + implementation support  
3) **Reliability** — Microsoft 365, identity, endpoints, backups/DR  
4) **Fast, friendly support** — a smooth, human client experience

---

## Tone of voice
### Do
- Use plain-English outcomes and next steps
- Be confident but not hypey
- Keep sentences short and scannable
- Use supportive language ("We’ll help you…")

### Don’t
- Overuse buzzwords ("digital transformation")
- Make compliance promises you can’t verify
- Sound overly technical in hero sections

---

## Copy rules (website)
- **Headlines:** outcome-first, 8–12 words max  
- **Subheads:** 1–2 short sentences  
- **Bullets:** 3–6 bullets per section; prefer verbs (“Reduce…”, “Standardize…”)  
- **No hype:** confident, calm, specific

### Hero headline options
- Fix your processes. Strengthen your IT. Get more time back.
- Stop operational chaos. Build systems that scale.
- Reliable IT and clear workflows—built for small business.

### Hero subhead (recommended)
Polus helps Oklahoma small businesses run smoother by simplifying workflows, building SOPs, and improving Microsoft 365 + IT reliability—in plain English.

## Offers & packaging

### Free services (time-capped)
- **Free Discovery Call** (15–20 min) — clarify goals + recommend next step (no implementation)
- **1-page Next Steps Recap** — template-based recap with top priorities + recommended package + starting-at price
- **Process + IT Readiness Checklist** — 1-page PDF lead magnet

**Guardrails:** free = advice + next steps; anything requiring system access is paid.

### Front-door paid offers
- **Business + IT Systems Assessment** — **Starting at $249–$499**
- **Process Mapping + SOP Starter Pack** — **Starting at $1,250–$3,500**
- **IT Stability + Backup/DR Check** — **Starting at $399–$899**

### Service categories (site navigation)
- Operations Systemization (process mapping, SOPs, onboarding/hiring workflows)
- Microsoft 365 Systems (governance, identity/access, endpoint standardization)
- Reliability & Continuity (backups, restore tests, DR runbooks)
- Managed IT Services (Essentials / Plus)
- Web + MVP (lead-gen sites, MVP/PRD kickoff)
- Automation / No-code (workflows, forms → tasks → reporting)

---

## Proof & trust (new-company-safe)
You can safely use:
- **Certifications:** ITIL4, Network+
- **Framework-led delivery:** assessments, roadmaps, SOP templates, runbooks
- **Anonymized outcomes:** describe results without naming clients

Avoid until verified/permissioned:
- Client logos/names
- Testimonials (keep modules hidden until you have them)
- Compliance promises (HIPAA/SOC2/CMMC) unless you’re delivering them

**Preferred language:** “aligned with best practices”, “can help you prepare for”
