## Pricing Philosophy (Updated Jan 2026)

**Before Jan 2026:**
- Underpriced 30-60% below market rates
- Flat pricing without complexity tiers
- Minimal scope boundaries
- Lacked explicit assumptions and caps

**After Jan 2026:**
- Market-aligned pricing ensuring profitability
- Complexity tiers where appropriate
- Explicit scope caps and boundaries
- Clear "what's included/not included"
- Pricing adders for scaling factors
- Maintains Oklahoma market competitiveness

**Key Principles:**
1. **Transparency:** Show pricing upfront, no surprises
2. **Value-based:** Price reflects expertise and deliverables
3. **Boundaries:** Clear caps prevent scope creep
4. **Flexibility:** Tiers and adders for different needs
5. **Competitive:** Accessible to Oklahoma small businesses

```typescript
type ServiceData = {
  slug: string;
  title: string;
  description: string;
  overview: string;
  startingPrice: string;
  timeline: string;
  deliverables: string[];

  faqs?: Array<{ question: string; answer: string; }>;
  pricingTiers?: Array<{ name: string; price: string; description: string; }>;
```
